pumpkinpies

Descriptions:

This homemade pumpkin pie made with mashed, cooked pumpkin is my family's favorite.

Skip the canned pumpkin and store-bought crust and make your holiday pumpkin pie from scratch with a flaky homemade pastry crust and a deliciously spiced pumpkin filling. Hope you enjoy it as much as my family does!

Ingredients

  • For the crust: all-purpose flour, salt, shortening, and cold water
  • For the filling: pumpkin, evaporated milk, eggs, brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, salt

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and salt. Cut in the shortening, then add the water.
  2. Shape the dough into a ball. Roll it out on a lightly floured surface.
